FORGOTTEN PAST


There are monuments and sites which are lost to oblivion , the unparrleled charm of these places provides the nation with livelihood as well as esteem. But much to our dismay, even though these ageold marvels provide the region with fame and employment opportunities , alot less efforts are being put by the authorities to excavate them and provide these places their due glory. However, we have tried our best to bring the lesser known provinces to mainstream through this webpage. This webpage provides with obscure as well as lost sites of three countries, namely India , Italy and Egypt.
